Here are the details about Makemake

Makemake is the third largest dwarf planet in the Solar System and one of only two objects in the Kuiper Belt (KBO) [a]. Its diameter is about 2/3 that of Pluto. Makemake has a protective crystal that has been detected. Its extremely low mean temperature (about 30 K) means that its surface is covered with methane, ethane, and possibly glacial nitrogen.
