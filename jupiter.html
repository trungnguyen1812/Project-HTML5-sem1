Here are the details about Jupiter

The 5th planet from the Sun, Jupiter (Jupiter) is a very large planet, the largest in our solar system. Jupiter is a gas giant, containing mainly hydrogen and helium. The outermost layer of the atmosphere shows up with multiple bands of clouds at different heights, as a result of aerodynamic turbulence and interactions with storms at the edge. One striking feature is the Great Red Spot, a giant storm known to have existed for at least hundreds of years. Jupiter has a strong magnetic field, with dozens of moons around it, it looks like a miniature solar system.
